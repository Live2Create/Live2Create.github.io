Our Mission

Live2 is a movement that recognizes everyone in one way or another is an artist. We mean to inspire those who live to create by providing a network of supportive connections enabling everyone to express their artistic talents and reach an audience that appreciates their work. In a world of internet posts splattered with spam, we aim to provide a site that is driven by artists, for artists.

Our Vision

The world of art is quickly moving to become more and more centralized around interconnectedness. The ability to create using combined capabilities of many talented humans is a powerful development that the internet has developed a culture around. However, there has not yet been a brand that supports and represents these “interdependent artists” as well as celebrates the message of cultivating creativity and togetherness. The movement we are trying to start at Live2 is first and foremost one of aesthetics. We hope to appeal to the people that turn to art as an escape from the madness of reality; to the teens that finish their homework early or don’t do it at all just so they can work late nights on Garageband, Illustrator, or an open canvas; just to the people that just cannot live without creating new.
